About Us

We are a professional training institute focused on transforming learners into industry-ready professionals. Operating on a E-training model, we deliver high-quality live training using free & accessible tools such as Google Meet and Microsoft Teams. Our curriculum emphasizes practical execution— real templates, real tasks, and role-based simulations— so learners can confidently perform in corporate roles from day one.

Levario is built to help learners become effective contributors in corporate teams. Our focus is on practical application: recruitment workflows, HR operations, payroll basics, POSH compliance, performance systems, sales and BD execution, team leadership, and management fundamentals. We are a remote-first institute operating with low overhead so we can keep prices accessible while offering full live training and mentoring.

Our Goals

  • Develop job-ready skills for entry and mid-level corporate roles.
  • Provide practical, tool-light training that anyone can access.
  • Offer flexible, WFH-friendly schedules for learners across India.
  • Support career transitions with interview and portfolio prep.
  • Deliver industry-standard assignments, case studies, and assessments.
  • Maintain small batch sizes for personalized feedback.
  • Provide templates and workflows used in real organizations.

Why Choose Us

  • No expensive paid tools required — we use free, reliable apps.
  • Curriculum aligned to corporate expectations & KPIs.

100% Virtual — How It Works

Classes run live on Google Meet or Microsoft Teams. We combine short lectures, demonstrations, breakout practice sessions, assignments, and weekly assessments. Links and materials are shared ahead of sessions. All course work uses free tools so taking the course requires minimal setup.
